Research interests:

Working on the coastal oceanography and estuarine dynamics. My major technical approaches are numerical modeling and field observation. Topics interested to me are: plume dynamics, saltwater intrusion, numerical methods, sediments transport, and biogeochemistry modeling. In particular, I have done a couple of works on the Changjiang River plume.

Courses taught (at ECNU):

Numerical methods in ocean science, 2010; 2012; 2013; 2014.
